What is Yute?

Yute is a careers network connecting job-seekers with employers. You can think of it as 'Chat GPT for Jobs' with a range of features designed to help you find the right job. We collect over 100,000 jobs from entry-level to executive, and only match you to relevant opportunities based on your career preferences.

How does Yute work?

Yute uses an algorithm to match job-seekers with the right job based on their career preferences. Once you sign up and specify your career preferences, the app displays the most suitable jobs for you. With our AI Job Search, you can also use natural language to filter through the job and find exactly what you're looking for.

How can I create an account?

To create a Yute account, go to our website or download our mobile app. Click 'Find Opportunities', enter your email, fill in your career preferences, and you're ready to go!

How do I maximise my chances of landing a job?

To increase your chances of finding a job on Yute, ensure that your profile is complete and up-to-date, including your resume and career preferences. Also, make sure to regularly check the platform for new job postings, as we update our opportunities on an hourly basis.

Does Yute have jobs from outside the UK?

Yes! We are now live in over twelve countries, covering more than 100k jobs!

Is Yute free?

Yes, the main feature of Yute are free and will stay free for job seekers. The advanced features are paid.
